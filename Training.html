The start of an unforgettable journey in my aviation career. Instruction of the finest from my SPL (Student Pilot Licence ) up to my Private Pilot Licence (PPL) and Night Rating. By the end of 2013 I had achieved my PPl, with privelages allowing me to fly as a Pilot in command on a single engine aircraft carrying passengers privately and not for anyform of remuneration. Not long after in January 2014 I obtained my night rating and begun doing majority of my hour building towards my Commercial Pilots Licence (CPL). .
